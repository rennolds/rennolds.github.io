J.R. Rennolds Company fosters a culture of respect and teamwork, where every individual’s contribution is valued. From small to large scale projects within different categories, J.R. Rennolds Company presents daily opportunities to learn and grow. We believe in empowering our team members to reach their full potential and strive to offer career advancement opportunities to each employee.
We hire State of Michigan licensed electricians, including master electricians, journeyman electricians, and apprentice electricians, to fill the follow positions: Project Manger, Foreman, Field Electrician, and Apprentice Electrician. As an employer we seek employees who are willing to collaborate, are self motivated, career-minded, and wish to bring safety and professionalism to every project.

  • 7 Paid Holidays
  • 8-18 PTO Day’s
  • Bereavement Pay
  • Employer Sponsored Health and Dental Insurance
  • Carhartt Uniforms
