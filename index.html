J.R. Rennolds Co. has provided electrical services to a variety of construction projects in the metro Detroit area since 1992. From commercial, multi-family housing, residential, hospitality, light industrial, and institutional space; We strive to provide fair pricing, honor all schedules and deadlines, and work with our clients to provide budgets and value engineering when needed. Our staff of state licensed electricians are highly trained, and conscientious of each clients unique requirements. J.R. Rennolds Co. is WBE affiliated, and can meet requirements in regards to MSHDA, HUD Section 3, and Davis-Beacon wages.
